Topic 8: Number Neighbors (Successor & Predecessor)

Who Comes Next? Who Came Before?

Every number has neighbors! There's the number that comes right after it, and the number that comes right before it (unless it's zero!).

The Successor: The Next Number!

The successor is the number that comes immediately after a given number.

How do you find it? Just add 1!

  • The successor of 7 is 7 + 1 = 8.
  • The successor of 99 is 99 + 1 = 100.
  • The successor of the biggest 5-digit number (99,999) is 99,999 + 1 = 1,00,000 (the smallest 6-digit number!).

The Predecessor: The Number Before!

The predecessor is the number that comes immediately before a given number.

How do you find it? Just subtract 1!

  • The predecessor of 5 is 5 - 1 = 4.
  • The predecessor of 200 is 200 - 1 = 199.
  • The predecessor of the smallest 6-digit number (1,00,000) is 1,00,000 - 1 = 99,999 (the biggest 5-digit number!).

Important Note: The number 0 does not have a whole number predecessor (because we usually don't go into negative numbers here).

Finding Neighbors in Patterns

Successors and predecessors help us understand number patterns and sequences.

Example: 10,000, 10,001, ____ (The next number is the successor of 10,001, which is 10,002).
